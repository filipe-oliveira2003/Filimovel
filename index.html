Job Description

The real estate and general services analyst will assist the Global Head of Real Estate and General Services in the execution of the Real Estate roadmap, including, lease acquisitions and implementation of new ways of working.

Why join Filimovel?

Filimovel´s teams are dedicated to making an impact by harnessing data to transform the world’s most carbon-intensive industry into a force for good for the planet, and creating virtuous real estate. They embody our four core values: drive, commitment, righteousness and goodwill, which represent a fundamental element of life at Filimovel.

Our employees are at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to provide a comfortable environment where they can flourish.
